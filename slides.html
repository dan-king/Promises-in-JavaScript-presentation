A promise represents the eventual result of an asynchronous operation.

A promise is like the ticket you get after placing your order at a fast food restaurant.

Follow along at www.votingfun.com/sdjs

Three States of a Promise

  1. Pending
  2. Fulfilled
  3. Rejected

"Settled" means not pending

  1. Pending (not settled)
  2. Fulfilled (settled)
  3. Rejected (settled)

Being settled is not a state, just a linguistic convenience.
